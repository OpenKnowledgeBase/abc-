IGNOU Academic Bank of Credit (ABC) ID

1. Introduction

The Academic Bank of Credit (ABC) is a digital platform introduced by the Government of India. It allows students to securely store, manage, and transfer their academic credits earned from various recognized institutions. 2. Objectives

  • Provide a national platform to store academic credits.
  • Enable easy transfer of credits between educational institutions.
  • Support the multiple entry and exit system in higher education.
  • Ensure lifelong learning and credit accumulation for learners.

3. How It Works

ABC allows students to accumulate credits during their academic journey from different institutions and store them in a central repository. When students want to transfer institutions or programs, they can seamlessly transfer these credits. Here's how you can create your ABC ID:

  1. Visit the official IGNOU Website or the official ABC portal.
  2. Click on "ABC ID Registration" under student services.
  3. Provide your Aadhaar number for identity verification.
  4. Enter your academic details, mobile number, and email for communication.
  5. Submit the form to generate your ABC ID. You will receive it via email.

4. Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for the Academic Bank of Credit, you need to be enrolled in a recognized higher education institution in India that is affiliated with the ABC initiative. The program is open to undergraduate, postgraduate, and diploma students.

5. Benefits of ABC

  • **Credit Flexibility**: Transfer credits between recognized institutions.
  • **Permanent Academic Record**: A secure and permanent place for your academic history.
  • **Multiple Entry/Exit Points**: Leave and rejoin programs without losing credits.
  • **Personalized Learning**: Earn credits at your own pace and across institutions.

6. Implementation Guidelines

The ABC platform has been implemented in collaboration with the Ministry of Education and UGC. Educational institutions must be affiliated with the ABC system, and students need to create an ABC ID to participate in this credit system.

7. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the Academic Bank of Credit (ABC)?

The ABC is a national digital platform where students can store, manage, and transfer their academic credits from various institutions.

How does ABC ID help me?

ABC ID allows you to transfer and accumulate academic credits from different institutions and use them towards a degree or certification.

Is ABC ID mandatory for IGNOU students?

Yes, students are required to have an ABC ID to manage their academic credits and access various student services provided by IGNOU.

Can I transfer credits between universities?

Yes, ABC enables seamless transfer of credits between recognized universities across India.

What happens if I lose my ABC ID?

You can retrieve your ABC ID by logging into the ABC portal or contacting student services at IGNOU.
